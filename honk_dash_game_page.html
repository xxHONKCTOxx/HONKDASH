Honk Dash - Beta

Guide a goose through gaps between baseball bat obstacles in this fun, challenging game! Flap with SPACE, avoid collisions, and aim for a high score. The game gets faster and harder every 10 obstacles.

How to Play:

  • Press SPACE to start the game.
  • Press SPACE to flap the goose and navigate through gaps.
  • Avoid hitting the baseball bats or the screen edges.
  • Score points by passing obstacles.
  • In the Game Over screen, press SPACE to submit a high score or ENTER to restart.
